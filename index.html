Emergency Climate Agreement Reached Behind Closed Doors

Who: World leaders from over 20 countries

What: A confidential agreement on climate action

Where: Geneva, Switzerland

When: February 2026

According to internal reports obtained by CBC News, several governments have agreed on urgent climate policies that were not previously disclosed to the public.

The agreement includes strict emission limits and increased funding for renewable energy.

“This could be the most important environmental decision in decades,” said Dr. Alan Morris, a climate policy expert.
